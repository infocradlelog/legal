1. Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to users worldwide, with specific addendums for users in the European Union (GDPR) and the United States (California CCPA/CPRA & COPPA).

1.1 Architecture: Local-First & Zero-Knowledge

CradleLog is designed with privacy as its core feature. We adhere to a "Local-First" architecture:

  • Health Data: All baby profiles, feeding logs, sleep records, and photos are stored ONLY on your local device in an encrypted SQLite database.
  • No Cloud Sync of Health Data: We do not upload your health data to our servers. Therefore, we cannot sell, read, or share your personal health information because we do not possess it.
  • Encryption: We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption. The key is generated on your device and never sent to us.

1.2 Third-Party Services

We use limited third-party services strictly for technical functionality:

  • Supabase: Used ONLY for storing hashed Consent Logs (for legal proof) and anonymous Referral System metadata. It NEVER stores health data.
  • Google Firebase / Crashlytics: Collects anonymous technical data (app crashes, bugs) to improve app stability.
  • Google AdMob: For free users, we serve Ads. We request Non-Personalized Ads where possible, but AdMob may use device identifiers (Advertising ID) for frequency capping and fraud prevention.

1.3 QR Sync & Data Transfer

The "QR Sync" feature allows transferring data between devices using Bluetooth (BLE). This process uses End-to-End Encryption with ephemeral (temporary) keys. Data is decrypted locally, re-encrypted for transfer, and then purged from memory immediately. We do not facilitate this transfer via cloud servers.

2. United States Privacy Rights

Compliance with COPPA (Children's Privacy) and CCPA/CPRA (California Consumer Privacy Act).

2.1 COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

CradleLog is a tool intended for use by parents and guardians (Adults), not children.

  • No Collection from Children: We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. The data regarding your baby is entered by you (the parent) and stays on your device.
  • Parental Control: Since the data resides locally on your device, you maintain full control over your child's information at all times.

2.2 California Residents (CCPA & CPRA)

If you are a resident of California, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with specific rights:

A. Categories of Data Collected

We do NOT collect or store "Sensitive Personal Information" (Health Data) on our servers. However, through our third-party partners (Google AdMob), the following technical data may be processed:

  • Identifiers: Internet Protocol (IP) address, Advertising ID (IDFA/AAID).
  • Internet Activity: Interaction with advertisements and app crash logs.

B. Your Rights

  • Right to Know & Access: You have the right to request details about the data we collect. Since we are "Local-First," all the data we have is already on your device screen.
  • Right to Delete: You can delete all your data by simply uninstalling the App. We do not have a server-side backup to delete.
  • Right to Opt-Out of "Sale" or "Sharing": Under CCPA, showing ads can be considered "selling/sharing" data. You can opt-out by purchasing the Premium Version (which removes ads and tracking) or by enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" in your device settings.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

3. European Union - GDPR

For users in the EEA/UK, we rely on Explicit Consent to process data locally on your device.

  • Data Minimization: We do not transfer your health data to the cloud.
  • Right to Erasure ('Right to be Forgotten'): Uninstalling the app permanently erases all data.
  • Data Portability: You can use the "Export to CSV" feature to retrieve your data in a machine-readable format.
